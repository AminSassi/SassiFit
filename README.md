# SassiFit

A minimalist habit tracker for daily bodyweight exercise.

Build consistency with small daily workouts. Open the app, log your reps, close it. That's it.

## Features

- **Progress Ring** — See your daily progress at a glance
- **Exercise Tracking** — Push-ups, Sit-ups, Pull-ups, Squats (+ custom exercises)
- **Streaks** — Track consecutive days of hitting your goal
- **Heatmap** — Visual history of your consistency
- **Timeline** — See when you exercised throughout the day
- **Offline** — Works without internet after first load
- **Private** — All data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers.

**iOS:**
1. Open in Safari
2. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen

**Android:**
1. Open in Chrome
2. Tap menu → Add to Home Screen

## Tech Stack

- Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- No frameworks, no dependencies
- LocalStorage for persistence
- Service Worker for offline support

## Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any server. No accounts required.
